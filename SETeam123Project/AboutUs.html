We Care About Giving

Welcome to Noor

Noor operates as a non-profit, non-governmental organization committed to facilitating connections between altruistic individuals and those facing adversity. In a landscape where resources abound yet accessibility remains a challenge, we serve as the conduit bridging generosity and necessity, devoid of monetary transactions.

Our core mission is succinct yet profound: to empower donors and volunteers to directly impact the lives of individuals and communities navigating diverse challenges. Leveraging our cutting-edge web and mobile application, donors seamlessly navigate through various cases, each delineating unique needs and urgencies spanning from apparel to electrical appliances. With streamlined processes, donors confidently dispatch their contributions, assured of their efficient and effective delivery to beneficiaries.

At Noor, we demonstrate acute awareness of the manifold needs of both donors and recipients, reflected in our platform's comprehensive coverage of sectors such as health, education, refugee support, and orphanages. Whether the contribution involves blood donations, provision of educational materials, or assistance to those grappling with poverty, Noor guarantees that every act of benevolence reaches its intended recipient.

Our dedication transcends mere facilitation of donations; we are committed to fostering a community where compassion flourishes. Through our pickup and delivery services, reminiscent of the convenience offered by ride-sharing platforms, we ensure prompt delivery of donations to their designated destinations, affording donors the opportunity to track their impact at every juncture.
